Synthetic Foam Concentrate

High Expansion Foam (3% / 6%)

3%6%
LCF High Expansion Foam Concentrate is a synthetic surfactant-based foam concentrate designed for generating large volumes of foam with expansion ratios ranging from 200:1 up to 1000:1. This enormous volume of foam is ideal for total-flooding applications in enclosed or semi-enclosed spaces where conventional fire fighting methods are impractical or hazardous.

When released through high expansion foam generators, the foam rapidly fills enclosed spaces, displacing oxygen and suppressing the fire by both oxygen exclusion and cooling. It is particularly effective in spaces where personnel cannot safely enter.

Key Features & Benefits

Expansion ratio up to 1000:1
Rapid space flooding for enclosed area protection
Oxygen exclusion and cooling suppression mechanism
Suitable for total flooding applications
Can be applied remotely without personnel exposure
Effective on Class A and Class B fires
Low water usage compared to low-expansion foam
Compatible with standard high expansion generators
