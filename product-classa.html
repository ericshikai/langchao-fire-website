Synthetic Foam Concentrate

Class A Foam

0.3%0.5%1%
LCF Class A Foam Concentrate is a specially formulated synthetic surfactant concentrate designed to dramatically improve the effectiveness of water on Class A solid material fires. By reducing the surface tension of water to extremely low levels, it allows the foam solution to penetrate deep into porous combustible materials such as wood, coal, hay, rubber, and textiles.

Class A Foam is widely used in wildland fire fighting, structural fire fighting, and overhaul operations where deep-seated burning material must be thoroughly wetted and cooled to prevent re-ignition.

Key Features & Benefits

Dramatically reduces water surface tension
Deep penetration into porous Class A materials
Very low use concentrations (0.3% – 1.0%)
Prevents deep-seated re-ignition
Highly effective in compressed air foam systems (CAFS)
Excellent wetting and cooling performance
Biodegradable synthetic surfactant base
Suitable for wildland and structural fire fighting
