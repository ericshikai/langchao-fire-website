Fire Foam Concentrate

Alcohol Resistant AFFF (AR-AFFF)

3%6%
LCF AR-AFFF (Alcohol Resistant Aqueous Film Forming Foam) is a multi-purpose foam concentrate incorporating a unique polymeric membrane technology that forms a protective layer between the foam blanket and polar solvent (alcohol) fuels. This prevents the alcohol from breaking down the foam, ensuring effective and sustained fire suppression on both polar and non-polar fuel fires.

AR-AFFF is the preferred choice for facilities handling both hydrocarbon and polar solvent (water-miscible) fuels, eliminating the need to stock two different foam types.

Key Features & Benefits

Polymeric membrane resists alcohol fuel attack
Effective on both polar and non-polar fuel fires
Excellent film-forming on hydrocarbon surfaces
C6 fluorosurfactant — compliant with current standards
Suitable for low-energy and high-energy application
Compatible with all standard proportioning equipment
Long 10-year guaranteed shelf life
Stable in fresh, salt and hard water
