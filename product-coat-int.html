Fireproof Coating

Cementitious Fireproof Coating for Steel — Interior

Ready to use
LCF Cementitious Fireproof Coating for Interior Steel Structures is a water-based passive fire protection coating applied to structural steel beams, columns, and deck plates inside buildings. When exposed to fire, the coating provides excellent thermal insulation, slowing the rise in steel temperature and preserving structural integrity for up to 3 hours, depending on the applied thickness.

The cementitious formulation provides superior adhesion to steel surfaces, is compatible with standard primer systems, and can be applied by spray, trowel, or roller. It meets the requirements for fire resistance ratings under international building codes and standards.

Key Features & Benefits

Up to 3-hour fire resistance rating (thickness dependent)
Excellent thermal insulation performance
Superior adhesion to primed steel surfaces
Water-based — low VOC, easy application
Can be applied by spray gun or trowel
Hard, durable surface when cured
Compatible with standard anticorrosive primers
Meets international passive fire protection standards
