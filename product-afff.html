Fire Foam Concentrate

Aqueous Film Forming Foam (AFFF)

1%3%6%
LCF-AFFF (Aqueous Film Forming Foam) is a high-performance fire fighting foam concentrate consisting of a blend of C6 fluorocarbon and hydrocarbon surfactants with various solvents and stabilisers. Only C6 pure fluorosurfactants are used in LCF-AFFF formulations, ensuring excellent performance while meeting current environmental regulations.

AFFF utilises the unique film-forming effect to immediately cut off oxygen supply to the fire. The oleophobic properties of the foam enable a stable foam blanket to suppress reignition of the fire, making it highly effective for both fire suppression and vapour-sealing applications.

Key Features & Benefits

Rapid film-forming on hydrocarbon fuel surfaces
Excellent low-temperature performance down to -30°C
C6 pure fluorosurfactants — lower environmental impact
Long 10-year shelf life in sealed containers
Compatible with all standard foam equipment
Stable performance in fresh, salt and hard water
Outstanding burnback resistance
Low viscosity for easy proportioning
