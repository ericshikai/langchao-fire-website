What certifications do your foam products hold?▾
Our foam concentrates are certified to EN 1568 Part 3 (European Standard), ICAO Level B (aviation), CCS (China Classification Society for marine use), and MED/RINA per IMO MSC.1/Circ.1312 for Marine Equipment Directive compliance. We also hold ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and OHSAS 18001 management certifications. UL listing is in process for the North American market.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?▾
Our standard MOQ is typically 1 ton (approximately 1,000 liters). For sample testing, we can supply smaller quantities. For large projects, we offer competitive pricing on orders of 20 tons or more. Please contact us with your requirements for a tailored quote.
Do you ship internationally? What countries do you export to?▾
Yes, we export to 30+ countries including markets in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Southeast Asia, and South America. Our experienced export team handles all shipping documentation, dangerous goods classification (where applicable), and customs compliance. We ship via sea freight, air freight, or courier depending on quantity and urgency.
Can you provide samples for testing before ordering?▾
Yes, we can provide product samples along with technical data sheets (TDS), safety data sheets (SDS), and copies of relevant certification documents. Sample charges and shipping costs apply. Please contact us to arrange sample shipment.
Do you offer custom or private-label formulations?▾
Yes, our R&D laboratory can develop custom formulations to meet specific performance targets, climate conditions, or regulatory requirements. We also offer private-label packaging with your brand. Please describe your requirements in the inquiry form and our technical team will get in touch.
What is the shelf life of your foam concentrates?▾
Our foam concentrates have a guaranteed shelf life of 10 years when stored correctly (sealed containers, away from direct sunlight, within temperature range of -20°C to +40°C). A Certificate of Analysis (CoA) is provided with each batch confirming production date and key performance parameters.
Are your products PFAS-compliant or fluorine-free?▾
Our AFFF and fluoroprotein foam products use only C6 pure fluorosurfactants (not C8 PFAS), which have a significantly lower environmental impact. We are actively developing and can supply fluorine-free (PFAS-free) alternatives. Please indicate your regulatory requirements when inquiring and we will advise the best product option.
