Fire Foam Concentrate — Raw / Bulk

AFFF 100% Pure Concentrate

100% Undiluted Dilutes to 1% Dilutes to 3% Dilutes to 6%
LCF AFFF 100% is the undiluted, full-strength aqueous film forming foam concentrate, supplied in bulk for clients who require large-volume storage with minimal space requirements, or who wish to prepare working-strength foam concentrates (1%, 3%, or 6%) on-site by dilution with fresh or salt water.

The 100% concentrate is based on the same proven C6 fluorosurfactant formula as our standard AFFF 1%/3%/6% products, and meets all the same international certification requirements. It is particularly suited for large fixed foam systems where space for concentrate storage is limited, or for foam manufacturers and blenders who use it as a base for their own formulations.

Full technical data sheets, safety data sheets, and certification documentation are supplied with every shipment.

Key Features & Benefits

Space-efficient — 100% concentrate requires far less storage volume
C6 pure fluorosurfactants — EN1568, ICAO, CCS, MED certified
Dilutes to any working concentration: 1%, 3%, or 6%
Compatible with fresh water, salt water, and hard water
Ideal for bulk supply to large foam storage systems
Suitable as base ingredient for foam blenders
Consistent batch quality with Certificate of Analysis
Available in IBC, drum, or bulk tanker delivery
