Fluorine Foam Concentrate

AFFF — Aqueous Film Forming Foam

🏅 EN1568 Part 3 ✈️ ICAO Level B ⚓ CCS 🚢 MED / IMO

LCF-AFFF consists of a blend of C6 fluorocarbon and hydrocarbon surfactants with various solvents and stabilisers. The unique aqueous film-forming effect immediately cuts off oxygen supply to the fire, while oleophobic properties maintain a stable foam blanket to suppress reignition.

Applications

  • ›Oil fields, depots & storage tanks
  • ›Airport Crash Fire Rescue (CFR)
  • ›Marine tankers & offshore platforms
  • ›Chemical plants & loading facilities
