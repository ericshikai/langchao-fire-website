Who We Are

A Legacy Built on Trust

Luoyang Langchao Fire Technology Co., Ltd. is one of China's most experienced manufacturers of fire fighting foam agents and fire suppressants. Headquartered in Luoyang — the historic ancient capital of China — our company traces its origins to 1954 when it was established as a government-designated fire extinguishing agent producer.

Today, Langchao Fire operates a modern, ISO-certified facility with an annual production capacity of 200,000 tons. Our products are trusted by clients in more than 30 countries across the energy, aviation, maritime, chemical, and construction sectors.

We hold 8 major international certifications including EN1568 Part 3, ICAO Level B, CCS, MED, ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and OHSAS 18001 — a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Company Timeline

Our History

From a small state-owned plant in 1954 to a globally recognized fire protection manufacturer.

1954
The predecessor of Langchao Fire was founded in Luoyang, Henan Province — the historic ancient capital of China. Established as a state-designated manufacturing enterprise under the People's Republic of China.

1969
Officially transitioned to the production of fire fighting agents. Designated by the government as an authorized fire extinguishing agent manufacturer — marking the beginning of our core business.
1983
Recognized as a firefighting agent production enterprise of Laocheng District, Luoyang City. Expanded production capacity and began developing new product lines to serve the growing industrial sector.

2008
Restructured as a private enterprise and renamed Luoyang Langchao Fire Extinguishing Agent Co., Ltd. This transformation brought new investment, management innovation, and an export-oriented growth strategy.
2010
Upgraded to a shareholding company: Luoyang Langchao Fire Technology Co., Ltd. Began systematic international certification process to enter European and global markets.

2013
Foam products certified to EN 1568 European Standard (Part 3) — a major milestone opening the door to European and international export markets. First Chinese foam manufacturer to achieve this for certain product lines.
2017
Listed on the NEEQ (National Equities Exchange and Quotations). Foam products received ICAO (International Civil Aviation Organization) Level B approval — opening the aviation Crash Fire Rescue (CFR) market globally.

2019
Foam products approved by the China Classification Society (CCS) for marine vessel use — enabling supply to the global maritime and offshore energy industry.
2020
Received CE certification related to EN 1568 standard. Initiated UL listing process to enter the North American market. Continued expanding global distribution partnerships.
