EN 1568 European Standard — Part 3

EN 1568 is the primary European standard for fire fighting foam concentrates. Part 3 covers medium expansion concentrates for surface application on Class B (hydrocarbon liquid) fires. Certification demonstrates that our AFFF, AR-AFFF, and fluoroprotein foams meet stringent European performance criteria for fire suppression, burnback resistance, and foam quality.

ICAO Level B — Aviation Approval

The International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) sets global standards for airport fire and rescue operations. Level B approval is required for AFFF used in Crash Fire Rescue (CFR) operations at certified international airports worldwide. Our ICAO-approved foam concentrates meet the strictest performance requirements for rapid extinguishment and secure foam blanket on aviation fuel fires.

Issued By
ICAO — UN Aviation Agency
First Certified
2017
Application
Airport CFR — Global

CCS — China Classification Society

China Classification Society (CCS) certification ensures that our foam concentrates meet international maritime fire safety requirements for use aboard vessels registered under CCS classification. CCS-approved products are accepted by major international shipowners and operators worldwide, enabling supply to the global commercial shipping and offshore energy sectors.

Application

MED — Marine Equipment Directive (RINA)

Our foam products hold MED (Marine Equipment Directive) certification issued by RINA, approved per IMO MSC.1/Circ.1312 and IMO MSC.1/Circ.1312/Corr.1. This certification confirms compliance with the International Maritime Organization's (IMO) requirements for fire fighting foam used on vessels entering European Union ports and waters. MED is the CE mark equivalent for marine safety equipment, recognized globally.
